Email Policy

Policy Number: Effective Date:
12105 01 Aug 2004

Scope

This policy outlines the use of email. This policy applies to all employees who use or access the Internet.

Duration

Policy is permanent to be reviewed annually.

Policy

Email is intended to be used primarily for business purposes. Any personal use must be of an incidental nature, and not interfere with business activities, involve solicitation, commercial activities, and potentially embarrass .

  1. Employees shall use email to send documents to other staff connected to email system.
  2. Employees shall check mail at least twice a day and respond to messages promptly.
  3. Employees shall use email for official business only.
